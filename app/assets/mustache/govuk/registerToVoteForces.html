    Not what you're looking for? ↓

    If you’re a member of the armed forces (or the spouse or civil partner of someone in the armed forces), use this service to add or update your details on the electoral register.

    Before you start

    Registering takes around 10 minutes.

    You’ll need your National Insurance number (if you have one).

    Register to vote

    Or to apply by post, download the register to vote form (armed forces) [PDF, 128KB].

    If you're not in the armed forces

    There are separate registration services for:

    • non-service voters
    • Crown servants and British Council staff
